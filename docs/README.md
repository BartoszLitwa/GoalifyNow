# GoalifyNow -- Business Planning Documentation

GoalifyNow is a self-management SaaS platform that unifies goal tracking, training, diet, running, workout diaries, progress documentation, and social accountability into a single subscription-based web and mobile application.

The core premise: people today juggle 3--5 separate apps to manage their health and personal growth. GoalifyNow replaces that fragmented experience with one cohesive platform where users can plan, track, and share every dimension of their self-improvement journey -- and invite others to join them.

## Quick Summary

**What:** All-in-one self-management platform for goals, fitness, nutrition, and accountability.

**Who:** Health-conscious individuals 18--45, amateur athletes, gym-goers, runners, couples, friend groups, and personal trainers.

**Why:** The market is fragmented. Users bounce between Strava (running), MyFitnessPal (diet), Strong (workouts), Habitica (habits), and group chats (accountability). GoalifyNow unifies all of this with native multi-user collaboration.

**How:** Subscription SaaS (Free / Premium / Pro tiers) delivered as a responsive web app with mobile (iOS/Android) support.
